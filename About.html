About

Drawing is a universal language. Using this tool we aim to provide a space for open discussion about topics we find difficult to verbalise. Use this in workshops, discussion spaces, classrooms or the dinner table to explain your opinion of a subject digitially and compare with society's visuals of said topic.

This website will be live for 3 months, constantly collecting the opinions of our users anonymously. This content will be printed into a publiction archiving today's opinions. Back
